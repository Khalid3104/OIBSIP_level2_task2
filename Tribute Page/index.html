Rohit Sharma

Rohit is an Indian Cricketer and a right-handed opening batsman and bowls right-arm off break occasionally. Rohit is widely popular for his aggressive style of batting where he likes to take the charge to the bowler. He is the only batsman in the world to hit three double centuries in ODI cricket. He also holds the record for most number of runs scored by a batsman in a single innings of ODI cricket (264 vs Sri Lanka). Because of his aggressive batting style, Rohit has been nicknamed 'Hitman'.

Records and Honours

  • Highest individual score in ODIs - 264.
  • Only player with three double centuries in ODIs.
  • The highest number of centuries in a single ICC World Cup- 5
  • Rohit Sharma was honoured with ‘Arjuna Award’ in 2015.
  • Awarded the Golden Bat award for scoring most number of runs in ICC Cricket World Cup 2019.
  • Rohit Sharma was awarded the highest sports honor of the country, the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award in 2020
  • Captained Mumbai Indians to fourth IPL victory
